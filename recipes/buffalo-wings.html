BONELESS BUFFALO WINGS


INGREDIENTS (2 serv.)

  • Oil for deep frying
  • 2/3 cup unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/3 teaspoons salt
  • 1/3 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/3 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/6 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2/3 egg
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1/2-inch strips
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons hot pepper sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter

STEPS

  1. Heat oil in a deep-dryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  2. Combine flour, salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, paprike, and garlic powder in a large bowl. Whisk together egg and milk in a small bowl.

  3. Dip each piece of chicken in egg mixture, then roll in flour mixture. Repeat so that each piece of chicken is double-coated. Refrigerate breaded chicken for 20 minutes.

  4. Fry chicken in hot oil in batches. Cook until golden brown and juices run clear, 5 to 6 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).

  5. Combine hot sauce and butter in a microwave-safe bowl. Heat sauce in the microwave until melted, 20 to 30 seconds. Pour sauce over fried chicken; mix to coat.
